Welcome To: Guess The Song!

Test your music knowledge by guessing the song title from its initials.

Each correct guess is worth 5 points.

You have an additional two hints, the song's artist and the title of the album the song features on.

These hints can be redeemed at the cost of 2 points per hint.

Redeem only one, and a correct guess is worth 3 points. Redeem both, and a correct guess is worth only 1 point.

The game doesn't stop until you guess incorrectly.


Note: Capitals are not required in your guess.

Note: Punctuation has been removed too, meaning any commas, apostrophes, et cetera, are not in the titles.

Example: "It's The Song" can be guessed as "its the song".


Your points are saved into a leaderboard, keep guessing until you get the highest score!
